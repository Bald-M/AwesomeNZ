# Bank Statements

Visa applications, loans, rentals, and similar scenarios often require bank statements. This guide explains how to obtain electronic statements from different banks.

::: tip
Electronic statements can usually be downloaded directly as PDFs or exported as bank-issued documents. Confirm in advance whether the receiving party accepts electronic copies.
:::

## Covered Banks

| Bank | Applicable scenario | Link |
|------|---------------------|------|

| [Kiwibank](/en/bank-statement/kiwibank/) | New Zealand | [Kiwibank website](https://www.kiwibank.co.nz/personal-banking/) |

## Access Points

- **ANZ New Zealand**: log in to [ANZ Internet Banking](https://www.anz.co.nz/personal/accounts/online-banking/) or the goMoney App
- **BNZ New Zealand**: log in to the [BNZ website](https://www.bnz.co.nz/) or the app
- **Kiwibank New Zealand**: log in to the [Kiwibank website](https://www.kiwibank.co.nz/personal-banking/)
- **China Merchants Bank China**: China Merchants Bank App / online banking

## Notes

- Statements usually need to cover the most recent 3-6 months. Follow the specific requirements of the visa or receiving party.
- Some organizations require an original document stamped by the bank. You may need to print and stamp it at a branch.
- Make sure electronic statements are complete, clear, and traceable.
