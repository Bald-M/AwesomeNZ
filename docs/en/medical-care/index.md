# Medical Care

In New Zealand, everyday medical needs usually start with a **GP (General Practitioner)**. In urgent situations, you can go to an **Emergency Department**, **After Hours** clinic, or **Urgent Care** clinic.

::: tip
New Zealand's healthcare system is different from China's. After arriving in New Zealand, it is recommended to register with a GP as early as possible and learn where nearby emergency and After Hours services are located.
:::

## Emergency Help

- **111**: ambulance, fire, police (serious emergencies)
- **Healthline 0800 611 116**: free 24-hour health advice (call when unsure)
