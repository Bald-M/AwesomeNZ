# Emergency Care

When you have an urgent health issue or feel unwell outside normal hours, you can go to an Emergency Department, After Hours clinic, or Urgent Care clinic.

::: warning
For serious emergencies, such as life-threatening situations or severe injuries, call **111** for an ambulance.
:::

## When to Seek Emergency Care

- Life-threatening situations, severe injuries, sudden severe symptoms, and similar situations
- If you are unsure, call **Healthline**: 0800 611 116 (free 24-hour health advice)

## Common Emergency Care Options

| Type | Suitable for | Notes |
|------|--------------|-------|
| **Hospital Emergency Department (ED)** | Serious emergencies | Examples include Auckland City Hospital; waiting times may be long |
| **After Hours clinics** | Feeling unwell outside normal hours | Some pharmacies and clinics provide evening/weekend services |
| **Urgent Care** | Urgent but not life-threatening situations | Fractures, cuts, and other issues needing prompt care |

3. Add the corresponding link under "Emergency Care" in the sidebar in `docs/.vitepress/config.mts`
4. Submit a Pull Request
