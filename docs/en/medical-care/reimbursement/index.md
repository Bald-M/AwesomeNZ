# Medical Reimbursement

After seeing a doctor in New Zealand, if you have the relevant insurance, you can apply for reimbursement according to the policy. Common types include student insurance and travel insurance.

::: tip
Before claiming reimbursement, confirm your insurance type and coverage, and keep receipts, medical notes, and other materials.
:::

## Covered Types

| Insurance type | Applicable people | Link |
|----------------|-------------------|------|
| [Student insurance](/en/medical-care/reimbursement/student-insurance/) | Student visa holders | Usually purchased through the school; covers GP, some specialists, emergency care, and more |

## General Reimbursement Process (Brief)

1. **At the clinic/hospital**: tell the provider you have insurance. Some providers can settle directly with the insurer.
2. **Claim after paying yourself**: if you need to pay first, keep the Tax Invoice, Consultation Note, and other materials.
3. **Submit the claim**: submit through the insurer's website, email, or app according to their requirements.
4. **Wait for review**: payment is usually received within several days to several weeks.
